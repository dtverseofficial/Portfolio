About

A bit about me

I'm Devendra, building under the DTverse brand — a personal space where I turn ideas into real, working products that people can actually use.

My journey started with curiosity about how websites work, which quickly evolved into a passion for building interactive web applications. I specialize in web development, Python, and Android development, with a focus on practical products and realtime experiences.

Using Supabase as my backend of choice, I've built everything from anonymous chatrooms with presence indicators to comprehensive learning platforms with teacher dashboards. I believe great software should feel intuitive and respond instantly.

Beyond code, I'm deeply interested in UI/UX design and how thoughtful interfaces can make complex tools accessible. My work in edtech combines these interests—creating study tools, planners, and platforms that help students learn more effectively.
